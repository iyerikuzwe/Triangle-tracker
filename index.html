what is a triangle?

Definition of a triangle

A Triangle is a plane polygon with three edges and three vertices. It is one of the basic shapes in geometry with three straight sides and three angles.

Types of a triangle

  • Equilateral
  • Iscoceless
  • Scalene

what is an equilateral triangle?

An equilateral triangle is a triangle that has all 3 sides are equal

what is a iscoceless triangle?

a iscoceless triangle is a triangle in which two sides have the same length

what is a scalene triangle?

A scalene triangle is a triangle that has three sides that are different
