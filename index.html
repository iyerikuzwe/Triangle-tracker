Definition of a triangle

A Triangle is a plane polygon with three edges and three vertices. It is one of the basic shapes in geometry with three straight sides and three angles.

Types of a triangle

  1. Equilateral
  2. Iscoceless
  3. Scalene

what is an equilateral triangle?

what is a iscoceless triangle?
